# Working with Godot

## Note
While we do not have an official integration for Godot Engine the [Utopia-Rise FMOD-GDExtension](https://github.com/utopia-rise/fmod-gdextension) is receiving regular updates. We will do our best to support you however we can!

More info here: [FMOD Forums](https://qa.fmod.com/t/possibility-of-official-support-for-the-godot-engine/19333/21).
